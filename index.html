Who We Are

A School Built on Faith, Excellence & Community

All Nations International School was founded with a bold vision, to create an educational institution in Sri Lanka that draws the best from global teaching traditions while staying rooted in Christian values and love for community.

Our students don't just pass exams. They graduate as confident, compassionate leaders ready to contribute to Sri Lanka and the world.

Our Foundation

Vision, Mission & Values

Everything we do flows from a clear sense of purpose, rooted in faith and a commitment to Sri Lanka's future.

Vision

To raise virtuous patriotic leaders of Sri Lanka by educating students to reach their highest potential with Christian moral values.

Mission

  • Academically equip students to the highest standards
  • Nurture students' hearts, minds, and bodies to be healthy and upright
  • Integrate education to help students respect and accept people of different religions, cultures, genders, abilities and socio-economic backgrounds
  • Share our lives with students and be their mentors and guides

Admissions 2025 / 2026

Begin Your Child's Journey With Us

We welcome students from all backgrounds who are ready to learn, grow and contribute. Our admissions process is simple and transparent.

1. Submit Enquiry
Fill out the form or WhatsApp us. We'll be in touch within 24 hours.
2. Campus Visit
Tour the school, meet teachers, and see our facilities first-hand.
3. Assessment
A friendly, stress-free assessment to place your child in the right class.
4. Enrolment
Complete registration, submit documents and secure your child's place.

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate (original + photocopy)
  • Last school report / progress card
  • National ID / Passport of parent/guardian
  • 4 passport-size photographs
  • Vaccination records (Pre-Primary)
