Our People

Faculty & Staff

All ANIS staff and teachers work toward one goal, to produce virtuous patriotic leaders. Our teachers see their position as a calling, and work from their hearts. Our teachers do not see students merely as children, but treat all students as future leaders of our country. They are here to raise virtuous leaders, and they know the gravity of their roles and responsibilities.

All the staff and faculty at All Nations are constructed like a human body where every part is honorable and important. We are a team. We have a common goal we are running towards, and that is being faithful to Sri Lanka's future which has been entrusted to us.
